Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about the Clearvertise digital marketing dashboard.

What is Clearvertise Dashboard?

Clearvertise Dashboard is a comprehensive digital marketing analytics platform that consolidates campaign performance data from programmatic advertising, social media, and search campaigns into one unified interface. It eliminates manual tracking in spreadsheets and provides real-time insights for marketing specialists and client service teams.

How does the dashboard help with campaign management?

The dashboard centralizes all crucial metrics including Spend, Impressions, Clicks, Conversions, CTR, CPC, CPM, and CPA. It features intuitive color-coding (Green for on-track, Yellow for caution, Red for immediate attention) and automated pacing alerts to prevent over or under-spending while optimizing campaign efficiency.

What platforms does Clearvertise integrate with?

Clearvertise Dashboard integrates with major programmatic advertising platforms, social media networks, and search engines. The platform supports multi-currency campaigns and provides unified data visualization from all your digital marketing channels in one comprehensive view.

Can clients access their campaign data directly?

Yes! The dashboard includes granular user access level management, allowing you to provide clients with self-service access to real-time budget data and campaign performance. This enhances transparency, builds trust, and reduces inquiry volume while maintaining data security.

How does the budget tracking feature work?

The Budget Overview eliminates reliance on Excel sheets by providing immediate, clear breakdown of budget allocation versus actual spend. It supports multi-currency campaigns and offers real-time financial tracking that's visible to both internal teams and clients, preventing reconciliation errors and delays.

Is the dashboard customizable for different workflows?

Absolutely! Clearvertise offers extensive customization including workflow configuration for digital channels, formats, devices and strategies. You can configure platform-specific rates, margins, cost parameters, currency rates, and detailed pricing structures to match your team's specific processes and requirements.
