Perfect Solution To Master Your Media Plans with Real-time Control

The Media Plan control panel offers unparalleled insight into pacing and performance, eradicating the need for complex, error-prone manual tracking in spreadsheets. Previously, programmatic specialists spent hours each week updating intricate Excel files, leading to delays and potential missed optimisation opportunities due to outdated data.

Intuitive Colour-Coding

At a glance, assess campaign health: Green indicates on-track, Yellow signals caution, and Red demands immediate attention. This visual cue system guides immediate decision-making.

Automated Pacing Alerts

The dashboard automatically calculates and displays metrics like Budget Consumed, Impressions Delivered, and Clicks Generated against planned targets. This helps prevent over or under-pacing, optimising spend efficiency.

Enhanced Collaboration & Trust

CS teams can confidently explain performance to clients, using the clear visual cues and real-time data to highlight successes or propose proactive adjustments.

Transparent Budget Allocation

The Budget Overview eliminates the archaic reliance on endless Excel sheets for financial tracking. Previously, digital marketing specialists would manually compile spend data from various platforms, leading to time-consuming reconciliation and potential human errors. CS teams faced delays in providing clients with real-time budget updates, often resorting to outdated figures.

Our dashboard provides an immediate, clear breakdown of budget allocation and actual spend, visible to both your internal teams and your clients.

For Your Team

Instantly monitor budget consumption and identify over/underspend, enabling proactive optimisation.

For Your Clients

Empower clients with self-service access to real-time budget data, fostering trust and reducing inquiry volume.
